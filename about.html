About Us

Chakgaria East Sammilani Park Welfare Association is a community organization dedicated to preserving and promoting Bengali culture, tradition, and social welfare through its annual Durga Puja celebrations and year-round charitable activities.

We aim to bring the local community together through inclusive cultural events, and use the funds raised to support various development and welfare initiatives in our neighborhood.
