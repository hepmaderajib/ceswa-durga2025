Privacy Policy

At Chakgaria East Sammilani Park Welfare Association, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information.

1. Information Collected

We only collect the minimum required personal data such as your UPI ID or contact details (if you reach out to us directly). We do not collect card or banking information.

2. Purpose

Information collected is used solely for the purpose of recording your contribution, responding to your queries, or managing refund requests if applicable.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share or sell your personal data with any third parties. Your data remains confidential within the organizing committee.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable digital safeguards and do not store sensitive financial data. Payments are processed via secure UPI protocols through your bank's own app.

5. Contact

For questions about privacy, please reach out via the Contact Us page.
