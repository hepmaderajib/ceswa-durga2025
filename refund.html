Refund Policy

Chakgaria East Sammilani Park Welfare Association appreciates your contribution. As we use the funds for religious, cultural, and welfare purposes, we maintain the following refund policy:

1. Non-Refundable Payments

All payments made through this portal are treated as voluntary contributions or subscriptions. Hence, they are non-refundable under normal circumstances.

2. Exceptional Circumstances

In the event of duplicate payment or accidental transfer of a large amount, we may consider a refund request submitted within 3 days of payment. Proof of payment and a valid reason must be provided.

3. Refund Process

Once approved, refunds will be processed to the original mode of payment within 7-10 business days.

4. Contact

To initiate a refund request, please visit our Contact Us page and share transaction details along with your reason.
